Mission Statement:

The goal of the website is to promote and connect our exotic ice-cream flavors to customers and as well as get feedback from them in order to create and test new flavors for our enthusiastic ice-cream lovers as well as improve the current flavors

 

Success Evaluation

The website will be evualated through the following:

  • Checking whether there is an increase in sales/revenue
  • Comparing the increase in profit before and after the website
  • Feedback about websites from customers (bug reports)

Targeted Audience:

The website is mostly targeted to teenagers and young adults with enough income to spend on economic luxuries
